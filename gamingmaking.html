     New York continues to be one of the best places for aspiring game designers to learn their craft, with a decent offering of a variety of quality game design schools. In fact, New York state has one of the highest concentrations in the country of schools with games-related degrees, according to the Entertainment Software Association. However, New York doesn’t offer many traditional university options that rank highly compared to states like California or Texas.

     In the list below you’ll find all types of game design college programs available within NYC, from traditional 4-year schools to specialty schools, and even online options, but all schools listed offer accredited degrees.
