      Working on a game? Join with other game developers, artists, designers, producers, and general creatives coming together to discuss the technical and creative challenges in making a video game. Playtest days might be included.



Game Devs of Color Expo
515 Malcolm X Blvd, New York, NY 10037

      Game Devs of Color Expo (GDoC), a New York City-based gaming expo and conference designed to showcase the works and ideas of creators of color. What started as an idea during a roundtable at New York University transformed into a full-fledged conference and gaming expo.

The event is located at the the historic Schomburg Center for Research in Black Culture in Harlem, New York City.

Playcrafting NYC
1290 Avenue of the Americas New York, New York 10019

      NYC Gaming Playtest Night where developers will have a chance to show off their newest games one-on-one to the NYC Games Forum crowd. Developers will have the opportunity to pitch their games and get feedback from players there.
