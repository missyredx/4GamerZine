      Is just going to your local arcade bar still not enough to quench your gaming thirst? Then how about joining a meetup group or two... or three? Here are a few in NYC that might piqued your interest.



Brooklyn Legend - New York Based Online Gaming Community
Meetup at Xe May Sandwich Shop:
96 St Marks Pl, New York, NY 10009

      An informal group of adult video gamers, who have been playing online video games together, consistently for over 15 years. While primarily consist of East Coast, Brooklyn based gamers, their online community contains gamers who regularly play from across many different States and across many different platforms and games.

Switch and Play NYC
10 Rockefeller Plaza, New York, NY 10020

      Welcome to Switch and Play NYC! A group of Nintendo fans with Nintendo Switch systems that love to meet up, make friends, socialize with others, and play Nintendo games together as a community. They meet up on Fridays under Nintendo NY!

Esport NYC
Follow on Twitter
(https://twitter.com/EsportNYC)

      Located in New York City, their aim is to redefine esports on the East Coast. When fans truly love a game, they inspire greatness and their vision is to creates a space for inspiration to thrive and to be reached.
Whether you are just starting out and learning about esports or a pro, their Meetup is committed to supporting our East Coast Dreamers.
